Vision

Imagine a world where where what you consume revitalizes you and the planet, where nearly everyone has a high standard of living and a thriving environment, and the where the companies we work for leave everything they touch better. A world where both people and the planet are thriving.

This is the world we envision. Enable people and their environments to thrive, and we all rise together.

About

Zeal is a community and resource network for entrepreneurs and product leaders combining purpose and profit through #worldpositive companies: companies that solve social and environmental challenges, especially related to the oceans and the future of food.

We're a network of leaders actively creating a sustainable, inclusive, and purpose-driven world through business. We are entrepreneurs, artists, technologists, designers, scientists, investors, activists, business people, writers, thinkers, and doers.

In a finite world, Zeal exists to catalyze the most precious resource of all: conscious leaders with the will to act in order to bring about a world where people and planet are thriving.
